Privacy Policy

What we collect

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • Name
  • Contact information
  • Interactions with us
  • Billing or purchasing information

What it's used for

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • Create and process orders
  • Send orders for shipping
  • Process transactions
  • Personalise the user experience
  • Personalise product recommendation
  • Evaluate business performance
  • Understand our customer segments

Who we share with

Besides our staff, we share this information with:

  • Shippers in order to send orders for shipping

Opting out

Providing some information is optional

  • If you choose not to enter a shipping address, we'll be unable to send orders for shipping
  • If you choose not to enter billing information we'll be unable to process orders and transactions
  • If you choose not to enter email address we'll be unable to create an account for users, and allow them to use our services
  • If you choose not to enter email address we'll be unable to send customers email notifications, and newsletters

Additional info

We keep your information safe by having security protocols for creating, reading, updating and deleting data and only allowing staff to access the data .You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at admin@createbase.co.nz, or +64 211 543 805, or 16 Dapple Place, Flat Bush, Auckland, 2019, New Zealand.
