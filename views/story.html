Setting up Base

We're concerned about the stagnation of education systems in an accelerating world.

We live in a world where automation is synonymous with job security.

As technology propels society forward, there is an evergrowing concern for the sorts of skillsets required in future careers. The onset of the Technology Revolution gives rise to a necessity to educate critical thinkers and innovators. Yet, schools are struggling to keep up with the pace of modern demands.

And thus, CreateBase was initialised.

By creators. For creators.

In December of 2019, CreateBase Limited was started by students from The University of Auckland with the goal of making education affordable, accessible, enjoyable, and sustainably pertinent.

Learn about us
Our Vision

A world where people are hopeful that their problems can be solved and have the confidence to seek out those solutions.

Our Mission

To provide the means to decouple STEAM field opportunities from an individual’s social and economic background.

We have devised a double-pronged strategy.

Strategy A: STEAM Kits

We develop engaging STEAM Kits that capture and retain the interest of students. Our engineering kits provide hands-on experiences that promotes active learning beyond simply seeing and hearing. Students learn on their own terms.

Strategy B: 3D Printing-as-a-Service

We offer affordable 3D printing services for all purposes. It’s our belief that everyone has the capacity to create and we want to facilitate that process. So whether you’re a CAD expert, a hobbyist, or just someone looking to get into 3D printing, we’re here to provide you with everything you need.
